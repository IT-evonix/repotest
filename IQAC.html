Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) of the college was established on 01st July, 2004 with the prime task of developing a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of the College. During the post-accreditation period, IQAC has channelized its efforts towards promoting holistic academic excellence.

functions of iqac

key responsibilities towards quality assurance and institutional excellence

devising mechanisms for well-planned curriculum delivery and documentation.

preparation of academic calendar in consultation with authorities.

development and application of quality benchmarks for academic and administrative activities.

facilitating learner-centric environment and participatory teaching-learning process.

collection and analysis of feedback from all stakeholders.

conducting student satisfaction survey (sss).

dissemination of information on quality parameters to stakeholders.

organization of professional development and administrative training programmes.

documentation of programmes and activities leading to quality improvement.

acting as a nodal agency for quality-related activities and best practices.

development and maintenance of institutional database through mis.

conduct of academic and administrative audit with follow-up.

preparation and submission of aqar as per naac guidelines.

framing procedures for maintenance and utilization of facilities.

implementation of capability enhancement and development schemes for students.
