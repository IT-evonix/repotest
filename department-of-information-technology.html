About Us

K. V. Pendharkar College of Arts, Science and Commerce (Autonomous), established in 1979 and managed by Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, is a NAAC ‘A’ Grade accredited institution offering diverse programmes in Arts, Science, and Commerce. With autonomous status, the college promotes academic excellence, skill-based learning, research, and holistic student development in a dynamic educational environment.
