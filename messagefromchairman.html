Dr. Prashanth Rao

Chairman

Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the website of K. V. Pendharkar College of Arts, Science and Commerce (Autonomous), an institution nurtured by the vision and commitment of Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal. For more than five decades, our institution has been dedicated to providing quality education that empowers young minds and prepares them to face the challenges of a rapidly evolving world. The college has earned a strong reputation for academic integrity, student development, and its commitment to society.

At Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, we believe that education must go beyond the boundaries of classrooms. It must cultivate curiosity, creativity, discipline, and ethical values among students. Our aim is not only to impart knowledge but also to develop confident, responsible, and socially conscious individuals who can contribute meaningfully to the nation and the global community.

K.V. Pendharkar College (Autonomous) possesses tremendous potential to emerge as one of the leading educational institutions in the region. Our vision is to make the college the finest centre of higher education in the vicinity by attracting talented faculty, strengthening academic infrastructure, promoting research and innovation, and introducing contemporary programmes aligned with emerging global and industry needs. We are committed to providing world-class educational opportunities to the students of Dombivli and surrounding areas while ensuring that education remains accessible and affordable.

The autonomy granted to the college provides us with a valuable opportunity to design flexible, multidisciplinary, and skill-oriented academic programmes in line with the principles of NEP 2020. Through continuous improvement in teaching-learning processes, industry collaborations, and holistic student development initiatives, we aim to create an environment that inspires excellence and lifelong learning.

I invite students, parents, alumni, and all stakeholders to be a part of this journey of transformation and growth. Together, let us work towards building an institution that not only imparts knowledge but also shapes character, nurtures talent, and prepares leaders for the future.
