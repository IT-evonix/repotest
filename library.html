“Library is not just about lending and borrowing the books but it is a hub of all learning activities.”

Library of Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal’s K. V. Pendharkar College is among the large academic libraries in the district. Established along with the foundation of the institution, the library is well known for its enriched collection and remarkable user-centric services.

We have a fully accommodating library with 73,000+ books and Reading Room facilities. The library is automated using SLIM (Software for Library Information Management).

Library Services

For Students

  • Open Access: Students can browse and select reference books directly from the stacking area.
  • OPAC: Online Public Access Catalogue available for book search within the LAN environment.
  • Literature Search: Assistance provided for projects and assignments.
  • Internet Service: Free internet facility for academic use. Students must submit ID card and record their entry.
  • Book Bank Service: Textbook sets provided to needy and deserving students under Students’ Aid Fund.
  • Group Book Bank: Reference book sets issued to student groups (especially TY) for semester/year use.
  • New Arrivals: Displayed regularly on the notice board.
  • UGC-INFLIBNET-NLIST: nlist.inflibnet.ac.in

For Staff Members

  • E-Resources Access: Faculty can access research e-journals and e-books via INFLIBNET-NLIST .
  • Reference Book Updates: Department-wise lists shared annually.
  • Email Service: Notifications, new arrivals, and updates sent via email.
  • E-Library Services: Online searches conducted by library staff for research needs.
  • Internet Facility: Free internet access available for faculty members.

Special Library Activities

Reader of the Year Award

One of the oldest and most prestigious competitions conducted by the institution. Students apply with details of their reading interests, recently read books, and a critical book review. Selected participants appear for a personal interview before the selection committee to identify the best reader.

Readers’ Club

Established in 2019–20 to inculcate reading habits among students. Members meet regularly to discuss books and literary works. The club organized the programme “Shabdagandh” in January, where students presented poems on socially relevant issues. Currently, discussions are conducted through online platforms.

Book Exhibitions

Book exhibitions are organized periodically to create awareness about the rich library collection and encourage students to explore diverse academic and reference materials.
