NCC Girls & Boys Unit

The College has two NCC unit affiliated to 1 Maharashtra NCC girls and 3 Maharashtra NCC boys battalion. 80 girls and 56 boys can be admitted in these two units. Regular periods are arranged on Fridays. The students are allowed to participate in different levels of camps arranged by Battalion. Cadets appear for 'B' and 'C' certificate level exams arranged by NCC Directorate Delhi. Those cadets who complete the training satisfactorily are eligible to get 10 marks at College or University examination.

OBJECTIVES

  • To teach positive thinking and attitude to the cadets.
  • To mould the cadet into a united, secular and disciplined citizens of the nation.
  • Provides training to cadets which makes them confident, courageous, committed and competent leaders in all walks of life.
  • To provide a suitable environment to motivate the cadet to take up a career in armed forces.
  • Education and Recreations

PROCEDURE OF NCC

  • Enrollment of cadets
  • Conducting regular lectures and parade.
  • Collecting subscription from first, second and third year cadets at the time of enrollment and remitting it to Battalion.
  • Helping the cadets and motivate them to participate in social service activities, various camps conducted at various levels.
  • Motivating the cadets to undertake various adventurous activities.
  • Conducting competitions for selecting BEST CADET of the College.
  • Counselling of the cadets
  • Ranking the cadets according to their ability.

RULES OF COMMITTEE

NCC is a voluntary organization which is administered through the Ministry of Defence. The Defence secretary is overall in charge. NCC headquarter is situated at Delhi. Our College NCC unit is affiliated to 1 MAH /GLS/BN and 3 MAH / BYS/BN. Whose Group head quarter is located at Mumbai. There are 41 rules called the National Cadets Corps Rules, 1948 regarding enrolment of cadets, safety of cadets, promotion, Dismiss, Discipline etc. All the rules are strictly followed.
