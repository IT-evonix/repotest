Aided Programmes

Bachelor of Science: First Year B.Sc.

Compulsory Subject: Foundation Course - I

A student can choose any one of the following combinations.
(Two units of each subject in a group.)

  • Botany / Chemistry / Zoology
  • Chemistry / Maths / Physics
  • Chemistry / Physics / Zoology
  • Chemistry / Physics / Statistics
  • Botany / Chemistry / Physics
  • Maths / Physics / Statistics

Second Year B.Sc.

Compulsory Subject: Foundation Course - II

A student can choose any one of the following combinations comprising of two courses which he/she has selected at First Year B.Sc.
(Three units of each subject in a group.)

  • Chemistry / Physics
  • Maths / Statistics
  • Botany / Chemistry
  • Physics / Statistics
  • Chemistry / Zoology
  • Maths / Physics
  • Botany / Zoology

(* For Biotechnology & Computer Science refer to Unaided section.)

Third Year B.Sc.

A student can register for any one of the Science subjects which he/she has offered at the Second Year B.Sc. There shall be an applied component subject.

Applied Component

  • Chemistry - Drugs & Dyes
  • Botany – Horticulture & Gardening
  • Physics – Electronic Instrumentation
  • Zoology – Economic Entomology
