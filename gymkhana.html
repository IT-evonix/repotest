Rules to be followed by students in Gymkhana:

  • Students are expected to wear their identity cards in gymkhana.
  • Use of mobile phone is prohibited
  • Students will not be able to use the gymkhana facilities during their lectures/practicals
  • Strict action will be taken against students who do not maintain discipline during the activities.
  • For using gymkhana facility students are required to enter their names in the provided register.
  • Students have to produce their ID / fee receipt to obtain the equipment
  • Students are supposed to take care of the given equipment. If any loss or damage of the same then they would have to pay for it.
