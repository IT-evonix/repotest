Career and placement cell of the college grooms students and helps them be employable and make them ready to face corporate selection procedures. Cell provides Job opportunities to students and arranges for campus drives, summer placements & internships

We also understand the industry requirements make students match the same with our various industry centric programs.

We are deeply thankful to our recruiters and training partners for their endless efforts to make students employable and help society at large.

Career & placement cell conducts programs & sessions on the following areas

Aptitude Training

Career Counselling

Soft Skill Development

Personality Development

Objectives

01

To assist students in obtaining placement in industries, corporate sectors, and public sectors.

02

To identify the needs and expectations of employers regarding recruitment.

03

To act as a link between students, alumni, and the employment community.
