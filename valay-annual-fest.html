Valay Fun, Frolic & Celebration

Valay is our annual college fest which is the most awaited event for the students during their college life. Every year we plan it at INTRA-COLLEGE or INTER-COLLEGE level. We organise different cultural activities during the college fest. This fest is organized by students for students. It is the time of celebration, felicitation, feasting, and festivity when students not only present their outstanding performances but also earn awards for their academic and co-curricular achievements. It is that time of the year when students combine, show their talents, and perform enthusiastically.
