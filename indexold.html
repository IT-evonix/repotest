Shri Prabhakar Desai

( Indian Hotelier, Businessman, Agriculturist and Chairman of Dombivli Shikshan Prasark Mandal )

Shri Prabhakar Desai was born in Walawal Village in Sindhudurg, during the British Raj on 24th September 1937, as the youngest son of Shri Ramchandra Desai, who was a landlord. Shri Prabhakar Desai dons many hats. He is an Indian Hotelier, Businessman, Agriculturist and Chairman of Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal ‘a self-made man’ He is married to Mrs Shubhangi Desai and has two children.

About Us

It was a dream and passion of Dr. Prashanth Rao and Dr. Upasni who brought together a few more like-minded people. Thus, fourteen elite citizens came together to establish Dombivli Shikshan Prasarak Mandal in 1972. Mandal’s first office was started at Shri. Desai’s residence at Narayan Krupa in Dombivli East.
