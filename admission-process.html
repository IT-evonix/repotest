DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR ADMISSION TO FIRST YEAR (DEGREE)

1 ADMISSION FORM
2 DULY FILLED COLLEGE ADMISSION FORM WITH PRE ADMISSION ONLINE ENROLMENT FORM
3 ORIGINAL S.S.C. MARKSHEET FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
4 ORIGINAL H.S.C. MARKSHEET FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
5 ORIGINAL LEAVING CERTIFICATE FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
6 ORIGINAL AADHAR CARD OF STUDENT FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
7 ORIGINAL AADHAR CARD OF PARENT / GUARDIAN FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
8 ORIGINAL PAN CARD OF PARENTS / GUARDIAN FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
9 TWO PASSPORT SIZE PHOTOGRAPHS WITH RED BACKGROUND
10 FOR RESERVE CATEGORY STUDENTS ORIGINAL CASTE CERTIFICATE FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES
11 NON CREAMY LAYER CERTIFICATE FOR VERIFICATION WITH TWO PHOTOCOPIES FOR RESERVE CATEGORY STUDENTS OTHER THAN SC / ST
12 UNDERTAKING FORM (ONLY BY THE STUDENTS AVAILING BENEFITS OF SCHOLARSHIP AND FREE SHIP)
13 MIGRATION CERTIFICATE IN CASE OF THE STUDENTS OTHER THAN MAHARASHTRA STATE BOARD
14 FOR RESERVED CATEGORY STUDENTS, CASTE CERTIFICATE SHOULD BE OF THE STATE OF MAHARASHTRA. CASTE CERTIFICATE ISSUED BY ANY OTHER STATE IS NOT VALID IN THE STATE OF MAHARASHTRA FOR PRIORITY IN ADMISSION
15 AFFIDAVIT TO BE SUBMITTED IN CASE OF GAP IN EDUCATION

Document Requirements for Students of Other Board / State / Nation
Taking Admission to First Year (Degree)

1 STATEMENT OF MARKS (EQUIVALENT EXAMINATION 10+2)
2 TRANSFER OR LEAVING CERTIFICATE (WITH THE COUNTER SIGNATURE OF DISTRICT EDUCATION OFFICER)
3 MIGRATION CERTIFICATE
4 PASSING CERTIFICATE
5 CERTIFICATE OF VERIFICATION FROM THE VARIOUS BOARDS / INSTITUTIONS

Document Requirements for Eligibility Cases for Students of S. Y. Classes
on Basis of Diploma Courses

1 STATEMENT OF MARKS (EQUIVALENT EXAMINATION XIIth.)
2 STATEMENT OF MARKS (FIRST YEAR / SEMESTER WISE)
3 TRANSFER OR LEAVING CERTIFICATE
4 MIGRATION CERTIFICATE
5 PASSING CERTIFICATE (FIRST YEAR / SEMESTER WISE)
6 CERTIFICATES OF ANY OTHER QUALIFICATION
7 CERTIFICATE OF VERIFICATION FROM THE VARIOUS BOARDS / INSTITUTIONS

Document Requirements for Eligibility Cases for Students of Other Board / State / Nation
Taking Admission to Second / Third Year

1 STATEMENT OF MARKS (FIRST YEAR / SECOND YEAR)
2 TRANSFER OR LEAVING CERTIFICATE
3 MIGRATION CERTIFICATE
4 PASSING CERTIFICATE (FIRST YEAR / SECOND YEAR)
5 CERTIFICATE OF VERIFICATION FROM THE VARIOUS BOARDS / INSTITUTIONS
